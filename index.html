What Did We Eat Now

is a crowd-sourcing calorie-counter. Its purpose (if you could call it that) is to provide a look at what and when different people eat!

First. use the search bar to look for what you ate.

Then narrow down your selection from the options below.

When you've found what you just ate, hit "I Totally Ate That!" and add it to the log!

To display this message again, click the above.
